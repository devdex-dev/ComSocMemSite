Who We Are

About COMSOC

The Computer Society (COMSOC) is the official club for all Computer Science, Information Technology, and related program students. We are dedicated to building a community of tech-driven individuals through education, collaboration, and innovation.

Our mission is to equip members with practical skills, industry insights, and peer connections that go beyond the classroom — preparing future IT professionals for the real world.
